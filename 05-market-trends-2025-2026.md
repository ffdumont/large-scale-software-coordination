# 5. Market Trends 2025-2026

## Research question

What are large organizations actually doing in 2025-2026 regarding large-scale software development organization?

## Questions to answer

- Are large organizations still using SAFe?
- Are they simplifying it?
- Are they replacing it?
- If so, with what?
- What do software vendors, cloud companies, banks, industrial companies, telecoms, and digital-native companies actually do?

## Preferred evidence

Prioritize:

- public experience reports;
- conference talks;
- engineering blogs;
- independent surveys;
- DORA reports;
- Thoughtworks Technology Radar;
- State of Agile reports;
- credible case studies.

Treat vendor material cautiously.

## Sectors to analyze

- large software vendors;
- cloud-native companies;
- banking and financial services;
- industrial companies;
- telecommunications;
- public sector and regulated environments;
- digital-native organizations.
